About us.

At the Pralhad P. Chhabria Research Centre, our goal is to accelerate scientific progress, solve challenging problems, empower the next generation of researchers in our country, and ultimately - help society.

Our Centers of Excellence

Vision

As a world-class academy to impart high-end education in Engineering and Technology the Research center has strived to meet the growing needs of the industry. Established on 10 acres of land, this research center equips students to accept challenges in the areas of Information Technology, Engineering and other niche areas of expertise.

Mission

PPCRC has always propagated ‘Innovation and Leadership’ as their mantra to bring ground-breaking ideas and provide a rock solid foundation for learning. With the vision and mission of providing quality education, the Institute is constructed by world-renowned architects with its uniquely designed dome as its temple of learning.
